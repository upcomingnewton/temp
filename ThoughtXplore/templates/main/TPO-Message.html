Note From The Placement officer

O ur mission at UIET has been to provide its students with the best professional career prospects in the emerging global workplace and to equip them for more effective and organised contribution to their chosen professional fields. UIET has already proved its mettle by being ranked at #31 among the best Engineering Colleges in India and has set itself apart as an institute of repute in North India. Every hardship teaches us many new lessons, making us more focused and resilient to face the competitive world. Hence we at UIET have set a very high standard of curriculum delivery and assessment, with its own internal policies of high attendance, punctuality, and presentations as part of every course work. A highly distinguished and research oriented faculty and year round industry interactions, students are also encouraged to participate in inter college events on areas of professional competence. We share a wonderful relationship with industry as they continue to seek our assistance in various projects where our students actively participate. We have always fulfilled the expectations of our past recruiters and hope to do the same this year. We encourage all private and government sector companies to participate in the recruitment process and we are sure that the students recruited will play a pivotal role in the growth of the respective organizations.

Dr. Manu Sharma

TPO, UIET
